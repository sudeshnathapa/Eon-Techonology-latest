PTZ Camera

A PTZ camera, also known as a pan-tilt-zoom camera, is a type of camera that can be remotely controlled to pan (rotate horizontally), tilt (rotate vertically), and zoom in or out. These cameras are commonly used for surveillance and security purposes, as they can cover a wide range of area and capture high-quality footage of specific locations or objects.

The movement of a PTZ camera can be controlled through a joystick, remote control, or software interface, allowing the user to adjust the camera's position and zoom in on specific areas of interest. Some PTZ cameras also have built-in features such as motion detection, infrared night vision, and automatic tracking, which further enhance their surveillance capabilities.

PTZ cameras are available in various sizes and specifications, ranging from small indoor models to large outdoor units. They can be mounted on walls, ceilings, or poles, depending on the application. PTZ cameras are widely used in a variety of settings, including public places, retail stores, office buildings, and industrial sites, to provide enhanced security and surveillance.

At EON technology, we provide Dahua PTZ cameras that provide high-quality video surveillance with the ability to remotely control the camera's movement and zoom features. These cameras can be operated through a variety of methods, including mobile devices, computers, and control centers.

Dahua PTZ cameras come equipped with a wide range of advanced features, such as motion detection, intelligent tracking, and image stabilization, which make them suitable for a variety of surveillance applications, including outdoor surveillance, public spaces, and critical infrastructure. These cameras also offer high-definition video recording, allowing for easy identification of objects and individuals in the captured footage. With their advanced features and versatile capabilities, Dahua PTZ cameras provide a reliable and effective solution for surveillance needs.
